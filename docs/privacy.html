Clip Johnson — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026.

Clip Johnson is a personal automation tool that posts video clips to the operator's own social media accounts. It is not a public service.

Information we handle

The app uses access tokens that the operator authorizes for their own YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok accounts solely to upload videos. Tokens are stored locally in the operator's system keychain and are never shared with third parties.

Data sharing

No personal data is collected from or shared with any third party.
